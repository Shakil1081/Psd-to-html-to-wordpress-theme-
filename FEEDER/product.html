Yard Feeder® Fertilize As You Water

All of your plants, whether lawn, shrubs, flowers or trees, need water and nutrients. The best lawn fertilizer delivers those nutrients consistently and safely every time.

Watch a 30-second video

Keep lawn care simple, grow more robust plants with less water.

With automatic fertlization systems, your sprinkler or drip irrigation system takes care of watering. Yard Feeder® takes care of the nutrients. As you water your lawn and shrubs, the water carries the nutrients into the root zone. This improves nutrient availability, so you often end up using fewer chemicals to fertilize. That’s fertigation, and nothing is more efficient.
